Series Queue.

First in, first out.

Customers are processed in the order that they joined the queue.

Accurate ETA

Customers get an updated ETA based on the average time each person ahead of them spends in one position.

Swap Positions

Customers can request to swap positions with any other customer behind them if they are running late.

Parallel Queue.

Stage Priority.

Customers are attended to according to their current stage on the queue.

Queue Stages

Queue stages are used to represent the current position of the customer (i.e processing documents, confirming payment, package shipped).

Queue Movement

Customers are updated every time they move to a different stage in the queue.
